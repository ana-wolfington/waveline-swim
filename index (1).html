Our mission
“Every child deserves to feel safe and fearless in the water.”
We started Waveline because we believe learning to swim is one of the most important things a child can do — and it should be taught by people who truly love the sport.

As champions who have competed at the district, regional, and state level, we bring real technique to every lesson. But what makes us different isn't just our credentials — it's that we keep things personal. Small groups, individual pacing, and a genuine relationship with every swimmer we work with.

Water safety isn't optional in Miami. We make it something your child looks forward to.

Programs for every stage

Little Dippers — Ages 2–5

Water comfort, breath control, floating, and kicking. The gentlest introduction to the pool, built around joy and safety.

Beginner Swimmers — Ages 5–9
